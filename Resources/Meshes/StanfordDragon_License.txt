Modifications to this model include:
- Importing into Blender
- Scaling down to much smaller dimensions
- Converting to natural Vulkan coordinate system (Z forward, -Y up)
- Converting winding order to CCW
- Exporting as Wavefront OBJ
- Converting albedo texture format from JPEG to TGA
